### Fig. 3 (1)

This figure is provided in the answer sheet and the contestants are asked to complete it. 

This image shows a glass device with **a square chamber at the top and a narrow tube at the bottom**.

The narrow tube is inserted vertically into a gray liquid at the base. The liquid rises inside the device, **filling the narrow tube and occupying the lower half of the square chamber**, thereby trapping a certain amount of gas (the white area) at the top of the chamber.
