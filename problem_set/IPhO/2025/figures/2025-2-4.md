### Fig. 4

This figure illustrates the graph of the imposed atmospheric pressure perturbation term, $P_1$, as a function of time. Specifically, $P_1$ varies in the form of a triangular wave: at time $t=0$, $P_1(0)=0$; subsequently, $P_1(t)$ increases linearly with a certain slope until $t=\tau_1/4$, where $P_1=A$; then, $P_1(t)$ decreases linearly with a slope of the same magnitude until $t=3\tau_1/4$, where $P_1=-A$; thereafter, $P_1(t)$ increases linearly again with the same magnitude of slope until $t=5\tau_1/4$, where $P_1=A$; followed by a linear decrease with the same magnitude of slope until $t=7\tau_1/4$, and so on, continuing to oscillate periodically in this manner.
