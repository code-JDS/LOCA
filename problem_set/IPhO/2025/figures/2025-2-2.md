### Fig. 2

Behavior A spans from $h=0$ to $h=H$, representing the transition from the top of the tube being flush with the water surface to the bottom of the tube being just about to detach from the liquid surface. The applied force $F$ varies linearly with $h$ throughout this process, ranging from $F=F_0$ at $h=0$ to $F=F_{\max}$ at $h=H$.

Behavior B also spans from $h=0$ to $h=H$. The variation of the applied force $F$ starts from $F=F_0$ and initially increases linearly with $h$ (for $0 < h < h^*$). When $h^* < h$, the force $F$ remains constant (maintained at $F_{\max}$).
